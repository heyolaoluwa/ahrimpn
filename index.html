Presidential Message

Abiodun Y. AFOLABI

National President, AHRIMPN

"Welcome to the new era of AHRIMPN. As we navigate the complexities of modern healthcare, the role of the Health Information Manager has never been more critical."

Dear esteemed colleagues, friends, and partners of the Association of Health Records and Information Management Professionals of Nigeria (AHRIMPN),

It is with immense pride and a deep sense of responsibility that I address you as the National President of our great association. We stand at a pivotal moment in the history of health information management in Nigeria — a moment defined by unprecedented technological advancement, evolving healthcare demands, and the urgent need for a robust, well-equipped workforce.

Our administration is anchored on four core pillars: Welfare, Standardization, Digital Innovation, and Advocacy. We believe that every registered practitioner deserves a safe, thriving career pathway; that the standards we uphold must align with international best practices including ICD-11 and global health informatics frameworks; and that the tools of the 21st century — from electronic health records to data analytics — must be accessible to every member.

Since assuming office, we have made significant strides in strengthening our institutional structures, expanding our membership base, and forging strategic partnerships with government agencies, academic institutions, and international bodies. We are committed to ensuring AHRIMPN remains at the forefront of healthcare transformation in Nigeria.

To our student members and young professionals — you are the future of this profession. We are investing in training programmes, mentorship initiatives, and digital resources designed to accelerate your growth and prepare you for leadership roles in the rapidly evolving health sector.

To our seasoned practitioners — your experience and wisdom are invaluable. We call on you to continue to mentor, guide, and champion the cause of Health Information Management at every level of your practice.

Together, we will build a digital future for health information management. Together, we will elevate the standard of patient care through accurate, timely, and well-managed health information. And together, we will ensure that AHRIMPN continues to be a voice of authority, excellence, and professionalism in Nigeria and beyond.

Thank you for your trust, your commitment, and your unwavering dedication to this noble profession.

Yours in service,
Abiodun Y. AFOLABI
National President, AHRIMPN
