About Us

Who Are
We?

The Association of Health Records and Information Management Professionals of Nigeria (AHRIMPN) is the nationally recognized professional body representing practitioners in Health Records and Health Information Management across Nigeria.

AHRIMPN promotes professional excellence, ethical practice, and capacity development, while advocating for effective health information systems that support quality healthcare delivery, research, and evidence-based policy formulation nationwide.

Our Story

Our Journey

A legacy of growth, reform, and digital leadership.

Formation Era

Establishment of a unified professional body for Health Records practitioners in Nigeria.

Professional Expansion

Growth of state chapters, national conferences, and training programmes across Nigeria.

Digital Health Era

Championing EMR adoption, data governance, and global best practices in health information.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We have integrity in our work and relationships. Our passion for doing the right thing and sharing knowledge makes us trustworthy, credible, and accountable leaders.

Honesty & Accountability

Bold

We act boldly with intention. We go after big ideas and challenges because we recognize the influence and impact of our work on people's health, safety, and well-being.

Purpose & Courage

Inclusion

We are inclusive so that humans are at the center of everything we do. We see the whole person, respect inherent dignity, and make space for different voices to get the best results.
